About Lucy

Photography rooted in emotion, connection, and meaningful memories.

The Heart Behind the Camera

Lucy DeWeese has loved photography since she was a young girl. Her passion deepened after two life changing moments: the sudden loss of her mother and the arrival of her daughter.

Through those experiences, she learned how powerful photographs can be. They preserve love, memory, and connection across generations.

Today, Lucy focuses on capturing authentic family moments that feel real, warm, and timeless.

My Photography Style

I focus on natural light, real emotion, and genuine connection.

My sessions are calm, relaxed, and centered on real moments rather than poses.
